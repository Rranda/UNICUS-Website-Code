About Us

We are a group of ambicious students from different fields such as : micro-biology , computer sciences , english language and literature , laws , economics and trade ; however our common point is the love of nature because earth is all what we all have in common , thus we decided to make a project out of it in order to protect it . ...We are UNICUS and our startup is about solving two major problems : Plastic Accumulation and Oil Depletion by making two smart machines : the first one is a smart bin to collect plastic and it serve citizens as well , the second one is a mini pyrolysis machine in order to turn 90% of that plastic into a friendly environment fuel.
